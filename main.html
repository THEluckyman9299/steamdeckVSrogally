Deck OLED vs ROG Ally Z1E

Rog Ally's advantages compared to Deck OLED

  • Z1E often delivers a 5-10% performance boost (can be even less powerful than the deck in certain scenarios)
  • Windows compatibility with many softwares such as editing softwares and Microsoft exclusives
  • 1080p, 120hz screen adjustable to 720p
  • Speakers have slightly more bass

Steamdeck OLED advantages compared to ROG Ally Z1E

  • Runs Native SteamOS
  • Track/touchpads with haptic feedback
  • User friendly
  • More comfortable
  • More back buttons
  • Better gyro calibration
  • Upto 2x the battery life
  • Much better in emulation
  • OLED , 90hz 1000 nits of brightness screen | 7.4 inch
  • SD Card slot that doesn't overheat and get destroyed
  • Plugins on decky can allow customization such as themes, limit battery usage, adjust fan curve, etc
  • Much more customizable
  • No annoying BSODs or updates

Disadvantages of ROG Ally Z1E compared to Deck OLED

  • Windows isn't always great
  • Constant blue screens and crashes (not Rog Allyâ€™s fault, but it's software not hardware)
  • SD card slot burns and doesn't permanently
  • Armoury crate updates
  • MyASUS updates
  • BIOS updates
  • Driver updates
  • Drivers for the Rog Ally aren't the best
  • AMD software adrenaline edition can stop working
  • SteamOS doesn't work natively on the ROG ALLY yet (Expect it to come out 5 months later, from 20th April to December unknown release date for a stable release)
  • Triggers start to squeak after long usage (6 months on avg.)
  • Battery life is abnormally short
  • Hands can start to ache after 30 mins of gaming

DECK OLED Disadvantages compared to ROG Ally Z1E

  • Compatibility for windows only softwares and applications
  • A bit less powerful
  • Screen with less resolution and refresh rate
  • Speakers don't have as much bass
  • Static on wired headphones
  • Layout of controller may take some time getting used to

Overall:

It really just depends on your choice.
If you play steam games, go buy a steam deck.
If you WANT to play Roblox and Minecraft, as well as a few multiplayer games natively, go for the rog ally.
If you don't wanna be scammed out of your warranty, go for the deck OLED.
If you love emulation, again, the deck OLED.
If you play modern AAA games, go for the rog ally. Just know the Steam Deck OLED can run modern AAA games, just maybe 10% less performance.
